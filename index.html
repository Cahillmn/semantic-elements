Semantic tags define the function and the category of your text, simplifying the work for browsers and search engines. It also makes it easier to read for developers. Semantics also helps with accessibility, searchability, internalization and other programmers to understand the structure of your webpage. Examples of semantic elements are header, footer, and article. div and span are not considered semantic.
